${0}: value of dataset first column

${1} is value of dataset second column

and so on

Example: the dataset:

your username 1,your password 1
your username 2, your password 2

Then:

$0 = your username 1/your username 2
$1 = your password 1/your password 2

${0} = your username 1/your username 2
${1} = your password 1/your password 2

Also support some base name case:

${0.camelCase} = yourUsername1
${0.pascalCase} = YourUsername1
${0.kebabCase} = your-username-1
${0.snakeCase} = your_username_1
${0.upperCase} = YOUR USERNAME 1
${0.lowerCase} = your username 1
${0.constantCase} = YOUR_USERNAME_1
